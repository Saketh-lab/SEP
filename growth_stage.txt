DataMesh is the leading data mesh infrastructure platform for Fortune 500 companies.

Problem:
Large enterprises have data scattered across 50 to 200 internal data sources — data lakes, 
warehouses, SaaS tools, legacy databases. Central data teams create catastrophic bottlenecks: 
6 to 18 month wait times to get a new data product into production. Meanwhile, business 
teams make decisions with stale or wrong data, and data engineers burn out managing hundreds 
of ad hoc requests. Data governance and compliance become impossible at scale.

Solution:
DataMesh enables domain-driven data ownership — each business unit (Finance, Marketing, 
Supply Chain, etc.) owns, publishes, and maintains its data as a product using our 
standardized platform layer. A central governance layer automatically enforces data quality 
SLAs, security policies, access controls, and compliance requirements (GDPR, HIPAA, SOC2) 
without requiring a central data team to be involved in every decision.

Customers reduce time-to-production for new data products from 8 months to 3 weeks. 
Engineering hours spent on data governance drop 70%. Data quality incidents down 85%.
