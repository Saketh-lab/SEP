Hi, we're EcoTrack. We help people track their carbon footprint.

Problem:
Climate change is bad and people don't know how much carbon they emit day-to-day. 
There's no easy way to see your personal impact.

Solution:
Our app lets you log your daily activities — driving, flying, food choices, shopping — 
and we calculate your carbon footprint automatically. You get personalized tips on how 
to reduce your impact. We also have a social feature so you can compare with friends 
and join challenges together.

Traction:
We launched on the App Store 3 months ago and have 2,000 downloads. 
About 400 people used it more than once in the past month.
We're at 0 revenue but thinking about a $4.99/month premium plan. 
We've also had one company reach out about using us for employee sustainability programs.

Market:
Everyone cares about climate change. There are 8 billion people on Earth. 
Even if we got 1% that's 80 million users at $4.99/month — that's $4.8B ARR.
The sustainability software market is growing fast.
